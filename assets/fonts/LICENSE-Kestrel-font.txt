Bundled fonts (font fallback stack):

1. Kestrel.ttf  — Noto Sans KR (NotoSansKR[wght].ttf, variable). PRIMARY.
   Source:  https://github.com/google/fonts/tree/main/ofl/notosanskr
   License: SIL Open Font License 1.1 (OFL).
   Covers Latin + Korean (Hangul). TrueType (glyf) outlines.

2. Latin.ttf    — Hack (Hack-Regular.ttf). FALLBACK.
   Source:  https://github.com/source-foundry/Hack
   License: MIT + Bitstream Vera. TrueType (glyf), Latin/monospace.

Both renamed for stable in-repo paths; unmodified upstream files. Used as a
glyph fallback chain (FontStack): per character, the first font that has the
glyph is used. CFF/.otf is not yet supported by the engine's font parser.
